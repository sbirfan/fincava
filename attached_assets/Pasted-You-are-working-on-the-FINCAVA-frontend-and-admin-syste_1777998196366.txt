--------------------------------------------------
CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------

Classify EVERY claim into:

A. KEEP
- Backed by real, live, verifiable data

B. REWRITE
- Directionally useful but overstated
- Needs early-stage positioning

C. HIDE
- Misleading or unsupported

D. ADMIN-CONTROLLED
- Valuable but should not be hardcoded
- Should be toggleable

E. NEEDS CONFIRMATION
- Cannot verify from code/data
