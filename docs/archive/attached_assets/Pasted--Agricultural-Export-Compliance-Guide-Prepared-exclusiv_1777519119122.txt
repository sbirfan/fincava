## Documents You Must Obtain

The following four documents are **legally mandatory** to export agricultural products from Colombia:

- ❌ RUT — DIAN Tax Registration
- ❌ ICA Producer Registration
- ❌ Phytosanitary Certificate
- ❌ DIAN Exporter Authorisation

---

## Your Action Steps

### STEP 1 — Obtain Your RUT from the DIAN

**WHAT:** Your official identification as a producer before the Colombian State. This document is the foundation of all subsequent steps.

**WHERE:**
- Online: [www.dian.gov.co](http://www.dian.gov.co) → "Tramitar RUT"
- In person: Nearest DIAN office in Bogotá

**WHAT YOU NEED:** Original national identity document (cédula de ciudadanía), an active email address, and basic information about your agricultural activity.

**COST: FREE**

---

### STEP 2 — Register Your Farm with the ICA

**WHAT:** The official registration of your agricultural property. This registration is mandatory before you may sell or export any agro-industrial product in Colombia.

**WHERE:**
- Online: [www.ica.gov.co](http://www.ica.gov.co) → "Registros y Licencias" → "Registro de Predios"
- In person: ICA Regional Office Cundinamarca — Carrera 41 No. 17-81, Bogotá

**WHAT YOU NEED:** Active RUT from Step 1, cédula de ciudadanía, proof of land tenure (title deed or rental agreement), and details about your crop type and productive area.

**COST: Between $0 and $50,000 COP**

---

### STEP 3 — Obtain Your Phytosanitary Certificate

**WHAT:** This certificate confirms that your products meet the sanitary and phytosanitary standards required by importing countries. Without this document, **your products cannot legally leave Colombian territory.**

**WHERE:**
- Online: [www.ica.gov.co](http://www.ica.gov.co) → "Certificación Fitosanitaria"
- In person: ICA Regional Office Bogotá — Carrera 41 No. 17-81, Bogotá
- Note: An in-person inspection by an ICA official is required at your farm

**WHAT YOU NEED:** Active ICA farm registration from Step 2 and full information about the specific product lot intended for export.

**COST: Between $80,000 and $200,000 COP per certificate**

---

### STEP 4 — Register as an Authorised Exporter with the DIAN

**WHAT:** This authorisation allows you to issue legal export declarations before the Colombian State and is the final requirement before conducting your first export operation.

**WHERE:**
- Online: [www.dian.gov.co](http://www.dian.gov.co) → Update your RUT to include exporter activity
- If you require assistance: engage a certified customs agent (agente de aduanas) in Bogotá

**WHAT YOU NEED:** Active RUT from Step 1 and valid ICA registrations from Steps 2 and 3.

**COST: RUT update is FREE — Certified customs agent fee: between $150,000 and $400,000 COP**
